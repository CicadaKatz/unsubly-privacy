Unsubly Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2025

Introduction

Unsubly is a Chrome extension designed to help you manage and unsubscribe from email subscriptions in your Gmail inbox. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data and protect your privacy.

Data Collection and Usage

Unsubly accesses and processes the following data to provide its core functionality:

  • Email Content: The extension scans your Gmail messages to identify subscription emails. This is done through Google's official Gmail API. We only access the following from the emails: subject, sender, date and body.
  • Subscription Management: We keep track of which subscriptions you have selected to manage or unsubscribe from. This information is stored locally in your browser and is essential to provide the requested functionality.
  • Local Storage: Subscription information and the last scan time are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

What we do not collect:

  • Email addresses (other than what's displayed in the email, and never saved).
  • Email content (beyond identifying subscriptions).
  • Usage data (how often the extension is used).
  • Personal data (other than what's necessary to identify subscriptions).
  • Data transmission (nothing is sent to remote servers).

Data Protection

We take your privacy seriously:

  • All data processing occurs locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device, except when interacting directly with Google's Gmail API.
  • We do not share your data with any third parties.
  • We do not collect or store any personal information beyond what's necessary for the extension's functionality.

Permissions

Unsubly requires the following permissions:

  • Gmail Access: To scan and identify subscription emails and to get information about them.
  • Storage: To save your subscription information and the last scan time locally.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can revoke Gmail access at any time through your Google Account settings.
  • You can clear all local data by removing the extension.
  • You can choose which subscriptions to manage or unsubscribe from.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
